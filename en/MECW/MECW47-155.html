Letter to Karl Kautsky, February 14, 1885

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 14 February 1885



First published in Friedrich Engels' Briefwechsel mit Karl Kautsky, Wien, 1955
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 47


ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY
IN LONDON[1]
[London,] 14 February 1885
Dear Kautsky,
I forgot to remind you day before yesterday that we are, as usual, expecting you and your wife to a meal this Sunday (tomorrow the 15th inst.), i. e. if you have nothing better to do, and you are hereby given a standing invitation to take potluck with me on Sundays.
Your
F. Engels
  1. ↑ Karl Kautsky lived in London from early 1885 to June 1888.
