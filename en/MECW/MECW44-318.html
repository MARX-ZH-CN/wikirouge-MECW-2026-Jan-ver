Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 20 September 1873



First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 33, Moscow, 1964
Printed according to a typewritten copy
Translated from the French
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 44


MARX TO EUGEN OSWALD
IN LONDON
[London,] 20 September 1873
My dear Oswald,
I have written to Wrôblewski,[1] who is as fluent in Russian as he is in Polish, being Lithuanian by birth.
If he does not agree I have another Russian (non-Polish) in petto.[2]
Yours ever,
Karl Marx
