Letter to an Unknown Addressee, June 19, 1871

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 19 June 1871



Published for the first time. Reproduced from the original
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 44

MARX TO AN UNKNOWN ADDRESSEE
IN [LONDON]
[London,] 19 June 1871
1 Modena Villas, Maitland Park,
Haverstock Hill, W.L.
Sir,
Please send me two copies of the Pall Mall of Saturday last (17 June).[1] I enclose 5 d. for the transmission.
Yours obediently,
Karl Marx
  1. ↑ The leader of this issue, 'The International Working Men's Association', dealt with Marx's Address The Civil War in France. See also this volume, p. 159.
