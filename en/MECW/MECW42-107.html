Letter to Salomon Fuld, November 9, 1865

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 9 November 1865



First published in: Marx/Engels, Werke, Bd. 31, Berlin, 1965
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO SALOMON FULD[1]
IN FRANKFURT AM MAIN
London, 9 November 1865
Dear Dr Fuld,
As I have lost my aunt's private address, I am taking the liberty of sending you the enclosed letter for forwarding to Madame Babette Blum.
Yours respectfully
Dr K. Marx
  1. ↑ On 16 July 1865 Marx's aunt Esther Kosel, who lived in Frankfurt am Main, died intestate. Along with other close relatives Marx was entitled to receive part of her legacy. Dr Salomon Fuld, a Frankfurt lawyer, was in charge of this matter.
