Letter to Eduard von Müller-Tellering, May 6, 1849

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 6 May 1849


Source: Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 38, p. 197;
First published: in G. Becker, Neue Dokumente von Karl Marx aus dem jahre 1849, Zeitschrift für Geschichtswissenschaft, 1974.
Collection(s): Zeitschrift für Geschichtswissenschaft

To Eduard von Müller-Tellering in Breslau

Harburg, 6 May 1849

Dear Tellering,

I warmly recommend to you the bearer, Mr. Bruhn, who is one of our principal agitators in Germany.

Yours very truly

Dr Karl Marx
