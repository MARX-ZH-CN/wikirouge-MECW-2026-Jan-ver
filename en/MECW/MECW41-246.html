Letter to Ferdinand Lassalle, August 21, 1862

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 21 August 1862


First published in: F. Lassalle. Nachgelassene Briefe und Schriften, Bd. III, Stuttgart-Berlin 1922. Published in English in full for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 41


ENGELS TO FERDINAND LASSALLE[1]

IN BERLIN

Manchester, 21 August 1862

Dear Lassalle,

As requested by you, I hereby confirm (indeed this goes without saying) that, 8 days before due date, I shall send you in Berlin the covering amount in respect of the bill I drew on you for 400 talers maturing 13 November 1862, and, if you will let me know at which banker's this sum is to be paid in, I shall see that it is paid in there by 5 November.

With most cordial regards,

Your

F. Engels
