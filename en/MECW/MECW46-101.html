Letter to Lev Hartmann, end of December 1881

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written December 1881


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVII, Moscow, 1935
Printed according to the original
Translated from the Russian
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46


ENGELS TO LEV HARTMANN

IN LONDON

[Draft]

[London, end of December 1881]

I have a letter from America for you, but I am instructed to hand it to you personally.

Can you come and receive it?
