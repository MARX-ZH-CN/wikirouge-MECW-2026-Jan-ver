Letter to Pasquale Martignetti, June 19, 1891

Author(s) Friedrich Engels

First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 38, Moscow, 1965
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 49


ENGELS TO PASQUALE MARTIGNETTI
IN BERGAMO
London, 19 June 1891
Dear Friend,
Today I am sending you
1. registered, your manuscript of the translation of Wage Labour and Capital,[1]
2. corrected proofs of the new edition[2] separately by book post,
3. Labriola's letter enclosed herewith.
I'm busy revising the new edition of the Origin of the Family, etc. which will have numerous additions.
A new, complete edition of Wage Labour and Capital[3] will follow as soon as I have got one. The principal additions are marked in pencil on the proofs.
I trust you are getting on all right in Bergamo; l'è una bella città, la conossi — l'ha appresi di parla / Meneghin, lu?"[4] Saludi
F. Engels
  1. ↑ K. Marx, Capitale e salaria colla biografia dell'autore e con una introduzione di F. Engels. Prima traduzione italiana di P. Martignetti.
  2. ↑ the fourth German edition of The Origin of the Family, Private Property and the State.
  3. ↑ K. Marx, Lohnarbeit und Kapital. Separat-Abdruck aus der Neuen Rheinischen Zeitung vom Jahre 1849. Mit einer Einleitung von Friedrich Engels.
  4. ↑ It's a beautiful town, I know it—have you learnt how to talk like the Milanese?
