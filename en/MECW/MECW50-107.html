Letter to Emma Engels, August 23, 1893

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 23 August 1893


First published in Beiträge zur Geschichte der Arbeiterbewegung, No. 1, Berlin, 1979
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


ENGELS TO EMMA ENGELS

IN ST. MORITZ

Alpnachstadt, 23 August 1893

Dear Emma,

I am here today and will be in the Bernese Alps tomorrow. A week from now I will be back in Zurich. I hope the Engadin is doing all of you good. Greetings to everybody from

Your

Friedrich
