Letter to Philip Stephen King, July 27, 1880

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 27 July 1880


First published in the language of the original (English) in Marx-Engels Jahrbuch, Nr. 8, Berlin, 1985
Reproduced from the original
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46


MARX TO PHILIP STEPHEN KING

IN LONDON

[London,] 27 July 1880
41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.

Dear Sir,

Please send me: Part IV of Reports from Her M's Consuls.

Yours truly,

Karl Marx
