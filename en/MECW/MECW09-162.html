Departure of Editor Karl Marx from Cologne

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 22 May 1849



First published in the Neue Kölnische Zeitung n°116, May 22, 1849
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 9
Collection(s): Neue Kölnische Zeitung

Cologne. Yesterday morning Herr Karl Marx, the former editor-inchief of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung, accompanied by some of the other editors, left Cologne and went to the region of the Upper Rhine; his activity there will be as successful as it has been here.[1]
  1. ↑ On the stay of Marx and Engels in South-West Germany (Baden and the Palatinate) after being compelled to leave Cologne, see Note 386.
