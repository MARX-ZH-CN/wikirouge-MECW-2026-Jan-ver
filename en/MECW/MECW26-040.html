Letter to the Federation of the Centre of the French Workers' Party

Author(s) Frederick Engels
Written 18 March 1887


First published in Le Socialiste, No. 83, March 26, 1887
Printed according to the newspaper
Translated from the French
Source : Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 26
Collection(s): Le Socialiste

Engels addressed this message of greetings to the meeting organised by the Federation of the Centre of the French Workers' Party on the occasion of the 16th anniversary of the Paris Commune. It was read out at the meeting.

London, 18 March, 1887

Citizens,

I am with you in my heart to celebrate the 18 March.

F. Engels
