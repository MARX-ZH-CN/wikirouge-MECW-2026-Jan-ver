Letter to Friedrich Engels, December 23, 1860

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 23 December 1860


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 2, Stuttgart, 1913
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 41


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 23 December 1860

DEAR Frederick,

My best thanks for the wine and the £5. My wife finds the port excellent.

I am my old self again today. Keil writes from Leipzig to say that, immediately on its arrival, the book[1]sold 120 copies.

Richter (of the Reform) writes from Hamburg to say it's in great demand.

My wife sends you her best regards.

Your

K. Marx

  1. ↑ K. Marx, Herr Vogt.
