Resolution On Terms of Admission of Workers' Organisations to the International

Author(s) Karl Marx
First International
Written 22 November 1864


Written: Adopted by the Central Council on November 22, 1864;
First published: in The Bee-Hive Newspaper; No. 163, November 26, 1864
Collection(s): The Bee-Hive
I

That organised bodies of working men be invited to join this Association in their co-operative capacity, the amount of their contributions to be left to their means and discretion.

II

That societies Joining this Association shall have the power to elect a representative to sit on the Central Council, the Council reserving to itself the power to accept or reject such delegates.
