Letter to John Lincoln Mahon, Not earlier than July 11, 1885

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 11 July 1885


Published for the first time. Reproduced from a copy of the draft in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 47


ENGELS TO JOHN LINCOLN MAHON

IN LONDON

[Draft]

[London, not earlier than 11 July 1885]

6d. stamps enclosed. Will feel obliged if you send the one copy to America as per address herewith:

F. A. Sorge 1 copy 'Commonweal' Hoboken, N. J. from July lst.[1]

U. S. America

  1. ↑ See this volume, p. 298.
