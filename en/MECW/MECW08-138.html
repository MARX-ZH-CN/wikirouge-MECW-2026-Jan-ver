Decision of the Court Chamber Concerning Engels and Other Co-defendants

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Heinrich Bürgers
Written 23 November 1848



First published in the Neue Kölnische Zeitung No. 58, November 23, 1848
Printed according to the newspaper
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 8
Collection(s): Neue Kölnische Zeitung

Cologne, November 22. On November 21 the Chamber of the provincial court of justice in Cologne committed Dr. jur. Becker to the Court of Assizes. On the other hand, it decided with respect to the co-defendants Wächter, Bürgers, Engels and associates that the records should be laid aside until these gentlemen had given themselves up in custody.
Herr Bürgers followed this hint, stepped up to the bar and was acquitted.
