Letter to Pyotr Lavrov, February 27, 1871

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 27 February 1871



First published, in Russian, in Letopisi marksizma, Book 5, Moscow-Leningrad, 1928
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 44


MARX TO PYOTR LAVROV
IN PARIS
London, 27 February 1871
Dear Sir,
Lopatin has left for the United States and I have not yet heard from him.
I have the honour to be, Sir,
Your obedient servant,
Karl Marx
