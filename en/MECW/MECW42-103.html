Letter to Hermann Jung, September 30, 1865

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 30 September 1865


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXIX, Moscow, 1946. Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO HERMANN JUNG

IN LONDON

[London,] 30 September [1865]

Dear Jung,

I am expecting you tomorrow (Sunday) for DINNER <A VERY FRUGAL ONE»; I have also asked De Paepe and Becker/ Please be so kind and invite Kaub in my name. This morning I am so PRESSED BY BUSINESS OF ALL KIND THAT I CANNOT AFFORD TO WRITE TWO LETTERS.

YOURS FRATERNALLY

K. Marx
