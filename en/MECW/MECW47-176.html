ENGELS TO PASQUALE MARTIGNETTI

IN BENEVENTO

London, 13 June 1885

Dear Sir,

Kindly send me six copies of your translation.c These will be enough.

I hope you have received the letter I sent about ten days after the translation.[1]

Your devoted

F. Engels

I am also sending you a copy of the Manifesto of the Communist Party of 1847 (by Marx and Engels).[2] Old as it is, I believe it still deserves to be read.

  1. ↑ See this volume, p. 291.
  2. ↑ written in December 1847-January 1848
