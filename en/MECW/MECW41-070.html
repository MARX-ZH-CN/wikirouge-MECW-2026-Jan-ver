Letter to Friedrich Engels, May 28, 1860

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 28 May 1860


Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 41


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 28 May 1860

Dear Engels,

The enclosed is from Lassalle.[1] Let me know by return what you think I should write and tell him re Fischel.

I do not fall in with his suggestion about Berlin. Nothing yet from Lommel.

Your

K. M.

  1. ↑ Lassalle's letter to Marx of 24 May 1860 suggesting that Marx should testify at Eichhoff's trial in Berlin. For Marx's reply see this volume, pp. 145-55.
