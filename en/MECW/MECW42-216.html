Letter to Ludwig Kugelmann, April 16, 1867

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 16 April 1867



First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 31, Moscow, 1963. Printed according to the photocopy of the telegram. Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO LUDWIG KUGELMANN
IN HANOVER [Telegram]
[Hamburg, 16 April 1867]
Dr L. Kugelmann Hanover
I arrive this evening about nine.
Marx
