Letter to Henri Ravé, Mid-March 1891

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written March 1891



First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 38, Moscow, 1965
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 49


ENGELS TO HENRI RAVÉ89
IN POITIERS
[London, mid-March 1891]
1. Translate pp. 121-25 and 140-45 as a sample.[1]
2. Ten copies of the translation, otherwise no conditions.
3. Lafargue to send him Socialisme: utopique et scientifique.
  1. ↑ The passages indicated by Engels will be found on pp. 256, 272 and 273 of Vol. 26 of the present edition.
