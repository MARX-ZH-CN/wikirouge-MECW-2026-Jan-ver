Letter to Friedrich Engels, June 24, 1868

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 24 June 1868


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 43


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

London, 24 JUNE 1868

Dear FRED,

Unfortunately, the sickness of bcth girls turns out to be SCARLATINA and Tussychen has it in a very malignant form.

Salut.

Your

K. M.
