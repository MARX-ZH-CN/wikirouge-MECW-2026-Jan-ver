Letter to Philip Stephen King, July 7, 1869

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 7 July 1869


First published, in Russian, in Marksism i rabocheye dvizheniye XIX veka. Nekotoriye aktualniye problemy teorü i istorü, Moscow, 1988
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


MARX TO PHILIP STEPHEN KING

IN LONDON

London, 7 July 1869

Dear Sir,

Please send me 1) The Report on Emigration, just issued, 2) ditto: On Water Supply

Yours truly

K. Marx
