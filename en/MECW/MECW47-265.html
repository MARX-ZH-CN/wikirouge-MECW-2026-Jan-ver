Letter to Karl Kautsky, July 8, 1886

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 8 July 1886


First published, in Russian, in Marx-Engels Archives, Vol. I (VI), Moscow, 1932
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 47


ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN LONDON

[London, 8 July 1886]

Safely home 5 9 0—today called to see Guillaume-Schack, but she was out — hence, so far have not had the pleasure of making her ac- quaintance. 592 Schorlemmer is with us — shall you be coming over here tomorrow evening?

Your

F.E.
