Letter to Friedrich Engels, January 26, 1859

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 26 January 1859


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 2, Stuttgart, 1913
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 40


MARX TO ENGELS 3 6'

IN MANCHESTER

[London, 26 January 1859]

DEAR Frederick,

T h e £2 safely received; the manuscript[1] gone off; wrote ECONOMICAL REVIEW for the Tribune yesterday. More tomorrow, and SOMETHING VERY AMUSING TOO.

Your

K. M.

  1. ↑ K. Marx, A Contribution to the Critique of Political Economy.
