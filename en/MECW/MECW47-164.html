ENGELS TO EDUARD BERNSTEIN

IN ZURICH

[London, after 16 April 1885]

Dear Ede,

Thanks. Letter from August:[1] the parliamentary group, whose authority August contested, resolved unanimously after a 3 days' debate that the personal controversy be dropped and the non-personal be postponed until after the Reichstag sitting, and with this August concurred. So the news of a defeat was false; we have won all along the line.

Your

F.E.

  1. ↑ August Bebel
