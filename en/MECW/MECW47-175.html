Letter to John Lincoln Mahon, between June 10 and 12, 1885

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 10 June 1885


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 50, Moscow, 1981
Reproduced from a copy of the draft
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 47


ENGELS TO JOHN LINCOLN MAHON

IN LONDON

[Draft]

[London, between 10 and 12 June 1885]

Postage - - 6d. Stamps enclosed - 3.2

Please forward me the 2nd copy at once Nos. 1 to 5,[1] they are for a friend in America.[2]

If the above be not correct please let me know what else there may be to pay.

Yours truly,

F. Engels

  1. ↑ of The Commonweal
  2. ↑ Friedrich Adolph Sorge
