Reproduced from the original

ENGELS TO SERGEI KRAVCHINSKY (STEPNIAK)

IN LONDON

London, 5 November 1892 122 Regent's Park Road, N.W.

My dear Stepniak

Thanks for your letter of the 25th October. 41 I find that Adler's letter to you was left here and has not yet been returned to you, so I enclose it now. Kind regards to Mrs. Stepniak from Mrs. Kautsky and

Yours faithfully

F. Engels
