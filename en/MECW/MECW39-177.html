Letter to James Grant, Editor of The Morning Advertiser, August 31, 1853

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 31 August 1853



First published in Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXI, Moscow, 1929
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 39


MARX TO JAMES GRANT, EDITOR OF THE MORNING ADVERTISER[1]
IN LONDON
London, August 31st, 1853
To the Editor of The Morning Advertiser.
Sir,
You will much oblige me by inserting the following in Your highly esteemed paper.
Your obedient servant
Dr. Karl Marx (28 Dean Street, Soho)
  1. ↑ This is a covering note to Marx's article 'Michael Bakunin' written for The Morning Advertiser and published on 2 September 1853 datelined 30 August (see present edition, Vol. 12). It is extant as a copy in Mrs Marx's hand. The signature and address are in Marx's own writing
