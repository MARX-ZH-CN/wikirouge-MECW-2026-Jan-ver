Record of Engels' Speech at the Public Meeting of the First Congress of Rhenish Democrats Held at Cologne on August 13, 1848

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 13 September 1848


First published in the Neue Rheinische Zeitung No. 101, September 13, 1848
Printed according to the newspaper
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 7
Collection(s): Neue Rheinische Zeitung

FROM A NEWSPAPER REPORT

Engels from Cologne: A characteristic feature of the Rhineland is hatred of Prussian officialdom and dyed-in-the-wool Prussianism; it is to be hoped that this attitude will endure.
