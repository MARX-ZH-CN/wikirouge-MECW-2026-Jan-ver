Letter to Friedrich Engels, March 11, 1864

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 11 March 1864



First published in MEGA, Abt. III, Bd. 3, Berlin, 1930
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 41


MARX TO ENGELS
IN MANCHESTER
[London,] Friday, [11 March] 1864
DEAR Frederick,
Tomorrow I shall leave from Euston Station at 10 and arrive AT MANCHESTER ABOUT 5 p. m.
It WOULD BE FOOLISH to go on waiting for good weather. What has also prevented me from coming DURING THE TWO LAST WEEKS—SOME NEW AND UNEXPECTED FURUNCLES BREAKING THROUGH DIFFERENT PARTS OF THE BODY.
YOURS
Moor
