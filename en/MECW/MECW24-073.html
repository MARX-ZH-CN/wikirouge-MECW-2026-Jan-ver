Notice on the Death of Lydia Burns

Author(s) Frederick Engels
Written 12 September 1878


First published in Vorwärts, No. 110, September 18, 1878
Printed according to the newspaper
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 24
Collection(s): Vorwärts

I herewith notify my friends in Germany that in the course of last night death deprived me of my wife Lydia, née Burns.

London, September 12, 1878

Frederick Engels
