Letter to Kari Kautsky, June 27, 1892

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 27 June 1892


First published in Aus der Frühzeit des Marxismus. Engels Briefwechsel mit Kautsky
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 49


ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN STUTTGART

[London,] 27 June 1892

Our good Schorlemmer died peacefully this morning. I'm going down tomorrow.[1]

There will be an obituary in the Vorwärts.[2]

Your

F.E.

  1. ↑ Engels visited Manchester for Carl Schorlemmer's funeral between 29 June and 2 July 1892.
  2. ↑ F. Engels, 'Carl Schorlemmer', Vorwärts, No. 153, 3 July 1892.
