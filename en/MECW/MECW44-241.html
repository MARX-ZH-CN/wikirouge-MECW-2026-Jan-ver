Letter to Th. Smart & Co, after July 3, 1872

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 3 July 1872


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 50, Moscow 1981
Reproduced from the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 44


ENGELS TO TH. SMART & CO.

IN LESTER

[Draft]

[London, after 3 July 1872]

Mr Glaser is a highly respectable man well known in Brussels whom I have no doubt you will always find thoroughly straightforward and honest in all his dealings. As far as I know, he is not without some means and I should not hesitate, if I were in a position to do business with him, to trust him to the extent of a couple of £100.
