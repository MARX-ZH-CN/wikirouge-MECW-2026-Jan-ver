Message of May Day Greetings to the Austrian Workers (1893)

Author(s) Frederick Engels
Written 5 May 1893


First published in the Arbeiter-Zeitung No. 18, May 5, 1893
Printed according to the newspaper
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 27
Collection(s): Arbeiter-Zeitung (Vienna)

London. Greetings on May Day. Long live the Austrian proletariat.

Kautsky, Engels, Freyberger
