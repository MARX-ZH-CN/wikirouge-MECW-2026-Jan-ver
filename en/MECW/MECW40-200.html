Letter to Friedrich Engels, January 8, 1859

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 8 January 1859


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 2, Stuttgart, 1913
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 40


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 8 January 1859

DEAR Frederick,

Herewith letter from Freiligrath. (I had written him something about the Kinkel affair.) Prospectus for the Hermann.[1] Mr Willich's letter.

Salut.

Your

K. M.
