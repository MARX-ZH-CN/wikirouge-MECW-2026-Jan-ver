Letter to the Saragossa Congress

Author(s) First International
Frederick Engels
Written 6 April 1871


First published in La Emancipación, No. 44, April 13, 1872
Printed according to the news paper
Translated from the Spanish
Source : Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 23
Collection(s): La Emancipación

On the Saragossa Congress see Note 99. The telegram was published in English for the first time in The General Council of the First International. 1871-1872. Minutes, Progress Publishers, Moscow, 1968.

London, April 6 [1872]

The General Council and the British Federal Council greet the Congress of Saragossa. Long live the emancipation of the proletariat!

Engels
