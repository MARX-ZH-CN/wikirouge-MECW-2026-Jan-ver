Letter to Juliusz Bałaszewicz-Potocki, November 29, 1871

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 29 November 1871



First published, in the language of the original (English), in Krasny Arkhiv, No. 6, Moscow, 1924
Reproduced from the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 44


MARX TO JULIUSZ BALASZEWICZ-POTOCKIs76
IN LONDON
[London,] 29 November 1871
1 Maitland Park Road, Haverstock Hill,. N.W.
Dear Sir,
The French edition of the General Statutes and Rules will only be ready in a few days when I shall send you some copies.
I shall be glad to see you at my house after 6 o'clock in the evening.
Yours truly,
Karl Marx
