Letter to Paul Kersten, November 24, 1875

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 24 November 1875


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVI, Moscow 1935
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


ENGELS TO PAUL KERSTEN

IN LONDON

[London,] 24 November 1875 122 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Dear Mr Kersten,

I have just received your esteemed note of this morning[1]and hasten to inform you that I shall be pleased to see you and your friend at my house next Sunday evening at about six o'clock.

Yours faithfully,

F. Engels

[Engels' later note on Kersten's letter]

[18]75. Kersten has not been seen any more.

  1. ↑ In his note dated 24 November 1875, Paul Kersten asked Engels to arrange a meeting with Marx and Engels for himself and his friend.
