Letter to Achille Loria, December 3, 1879

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 3 December 1879



First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 34, Moscow 1964. Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


MARX TO ACHILLE LORIA
IN MANTUA
London, 3 December 1879 41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.
Dear Sir,
For reasons of health I was compelled to absent myself from London for a time. On my return today I found awaiting me your letter of 23 November and your work.[1] I hasten to acknowledge receipt and at the same time express my thanks to you.
Yours very truly,
Karl Marx
[2]
  1. ↑ A. Loria, La rendita fondiaria e la sua elisione naturale.
  2. ↑ The postscript is missing in the manuscript.
