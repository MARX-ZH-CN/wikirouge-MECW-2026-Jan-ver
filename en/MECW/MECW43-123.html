Letter to Friedrich Engels, December 6, 1868

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 6 December 1868


First published in MEGA, Abt. III, Bd. 4, Berlin, 1931
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 43


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 6 December 1868

DEAR FRED,

Please send me £3 by this Wednesday[1] (the day after tomorrow), since the gas bill, etc., are due, and I have promised to pay by then.

Salut.

Your

K. M.

So it is Lowe and Bright Co.![2]

  1. ↑ 9 December
  2. ↑ A reference to the formation of a new Cabinet under Gladstone. On that occasion, The Times featured an editorial which contained, among other things, predictions about the appointments likely to be taken up by Lowe and Bright (see The Times, No. 26300, 5 December 1868).
