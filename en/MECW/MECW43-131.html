Letter to Asher & Co, not before December 12, 1868

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 12 December 1868


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 32, Moscow, 1964
Reproduced from the original
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 43


MARX TO ASHER & CO.[1]

IN LONDON

[Draft]

[London, not before 12 December 1868] The only place where a few copies of Herr Vogt are still obtainable, is Berlin. At least, there were some to be had a few weeks ago. I shall write there and if a copy can be got, it will be forwarded to 11, Unter den Linden.

Yours truly

K. M.

  1. ↑ This letter is a rough draft of Marx's reply to the letter from the firm of Asher & Co., Foreign Booksellers & Publishers, 12 December 1868. It contained a request for a copy of Marx's pamphlet Herr Vogt, which was not available in London.
    The fair copy of the letter has not been found.
