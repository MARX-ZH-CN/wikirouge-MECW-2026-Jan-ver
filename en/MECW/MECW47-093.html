Author(s) Friedrich Engels

Draft letter
Published in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 47


ENGELS TO SARAH ALLEN
IN LONDON
[Draft]
[London, about 6 July 1884]
Madam,
In reply to your note of the 5th arrived to hand yesterday and an answer to which was retarded by my absence from town, I beg to say that I consider Mr E. Aveling quite a desirable tenant and have no doubt that you will not regret having let your premises to him.
