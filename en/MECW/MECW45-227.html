Letter to Friedrich Lessner, September 12, 1878

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 12 September 1878


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVII
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


ENGELS TO FRIEDRICH LESSNER

IN LONDON

[London,] 12 September 1878
Half past one in the morning

Dear Lessner,

My poor wife[1]has just been released by death from her long sufferings.

I cannot send you the wine. However, you can have it fetched at any time.

Your

F. E.
