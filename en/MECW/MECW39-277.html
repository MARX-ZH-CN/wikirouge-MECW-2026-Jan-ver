Letter to Friedrich Engels, June 15, 1855

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 15 June 1855


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 2, Stuttgart 1913
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 39


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

London, 15 June [1855] 28 Dean Street, Soho

Dear Engels,

As you will have seen in an earlier no. of the Augsburg Allgemeine,[1]as from next August, the great Herzen will be bringing out a Russian periodical here, the Polar Star.[2]

My warm regards to Lupus.

Your

K. M.

  1. ↑ 'Großbritannien. London, 6 Mai', Allgemeine Zeitung, No. 130, 10 May 1855.
  2. ↑ Поляриая звезда
