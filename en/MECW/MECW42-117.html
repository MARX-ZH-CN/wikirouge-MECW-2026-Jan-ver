Letter to Friedrich Engels, December 2, 1865

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 2 December 1865


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 3, Stuttgart, 1913
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London, 2 December 1865]

Dear FRED,

I found your letter just as I arrived home (I was at the Museum[1]where I had some things to look up). I've just TIME to acknowledge the £10 with best thanks. I shall write more tomorrow.

Salut and COMPLIMENTS to MRS Lizzy.[2]

Your

K. M.
