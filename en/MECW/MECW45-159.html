Letter to Philip Stephen King, April 14, 1877

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 14 April 1877


First published, in the language of the original (English) and in German, in Marx-Engels-Jahrbuch, Nr. 8, Berlin, 1985
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


MARX TO PHILIP STEPHEN KING

IN LONDON

[London,] 14 April 1877
41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.

Dear Sir,

Please send me the Papers: The Greek United Church in Russia just laid before Parliament. Enclosed for account sent £1, 3, 3.

Yours truly,

Karl Marx
