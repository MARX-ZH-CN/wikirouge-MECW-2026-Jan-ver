Announcement of the Responsible Publishers of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung (2)

TO OUR SUBSCRIBERS
During the state of siege imposed on Cologne, when the pen has to submit to the sabre, the
NEUE RHEINISCHE ZEITUNG has been forbidden to appear and for the time being is unable to fulfil its obligations to its esteemed subscribers.
We hope, however, that the exceptional situation will continue only for a few days more, and then during the month of October we shall be able to ensure the dispatch of our newspaper to our subscribers in an enlarged format, with new powerful means for its support, the more punctually because before long the printing will be done by a new rapid printing-press.
Cologne, September 28, 1848
THE RESPONSIBLE PUBLISHERS
