Letter to John Lincoln Mahon, November 28, 1884

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 28 November 1884


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVII, Moscow, 1935
Reproduced from the original
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 47


ENGELS TO JOHN LINCOLN MAHON

IN LONDON

[London,] 28 November 1884
122 Regent's Park Road, N.W.

Dear Sir,

I received your note only this morning owing to the No. being stated wrong on the address (132 instead of 122).

If you will be good enough to call on me to-morrow Friday night from 7 to 8, I shall be glad to hear what you have to communicate to me.

Yours truly,

F. Engels
