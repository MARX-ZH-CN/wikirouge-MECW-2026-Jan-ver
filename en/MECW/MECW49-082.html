Letter to Henri Ravé, March 6, 1891

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 6 March 1891


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 38, Moscow 1970
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 49


ENGELS TO HENRI RAVE

IN POITIERS

[Notes]

[London,] 6 March [1891]

To send proof-sheet or two of his translation of Bebel.[1] Has he a publisher? No promise. Might indicate him the chapters[2] which will not be changed or not much so that he might begin. No promise.

  1. ↑ A. Bebel, Die Frau in der Vergangenheit, Gegenwart und Zukunft and La Femme dans le passé, le présent & l'avenir
  2. ↑ Of The Origin of the Family, Private Property and the State
