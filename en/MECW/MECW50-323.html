Letter to Wilhelm Saint-Paul, February 1843

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written February 1843



First published in Marx-Engels-Jahrbuch 1, Berlin, 1978
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50

TO WILHELM SAINT-PAUL[1] IN COLOGNE
[Cologne, February 1843]
Your Honour,
I am sending you this pamphlet[2] with the request to grant permission for its publication as a supplement to our newspaper.[3]
Yours faithfully,
Dr. Marx
  1. ↑ Saint-Paul, Wilhelm (c. 1815-1852)—Prussian army officer, then an official in the Ministry of the Interior; censor of the Rheinische Zeitung in 1843. (MECW)
  2. ↑ See Note 624
  3. ↑ Rheinische Zeitung für Politik, Handel und Gewerbe
