Resolution on the Behaviour of Members of the General Council at the Congress

Author(s) First International
Karl Marx
Written 28 August 1872


Adopted at the sitting of the Sub-Committee on August 28, 1872
First published, in Russian, in Generalny Sovet Pervogo Internatsionala. 1871-1872, Moscow, 1965
Printed according to the manuscript
Translated from the French
Source : Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 23

The Resolution was published in English for the first time in The General Council of the First International. 1871-1872. Minutes, Progress Publishers, Moscow, 1968.

No member of the General Council should have the right to accuse another before the International Working Men's Congress until discussion of the election of members of the General Council.
