Resolution on the Convocation of a General Congress of the International in 1866

Author(s) Karl Marx
First International
Written 19 September 1865


Written: Adopted by the Central Council September 19, 1865;
First published: in Russian, in Generalny Sovet Pervogo Internatsionala, 1864-1866, Moscow, 1961

The Standing Committee recommended to the Central Council to agree to the following as a recommendation to the conference.

The Central Council shall in 1866 convoke a general congress unless unforeseen circumstances shall necessitate its further postponement.
