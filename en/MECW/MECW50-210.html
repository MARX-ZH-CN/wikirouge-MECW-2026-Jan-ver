Letter to Maria Mendelson, October 26, 1894

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 26 October 1894


First published in Russian in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 39, Moscow, 1966
Translated from the French
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


ENGELS TO MARIA MENDELSON

IN LONDON

London, 26 October 1894
41 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Dear Madame Mendelson, At last we are able to receive our friends—even though the house still bears as it were the marks of seige and bombardment—we will have the pleasure of seeing both of you on Sunday evening.[1] Louise and the doctor[2] send their greetings,

Yours sincerely,

F. Engels
