Letter to Karl Marx, December 13, 1882

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 13 December 1882



First published, in the language of the original (German), in: F. Engels, P. et L. Lafargue, Correspondance, t. I, Paris, 1956
Printed according to the original
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46


ENGELS TO MARX
IN VENTNOR
[London, 13 December 1882]
Just received this, 9.20 p.m. After appearing at Montluçon Paul[1] will, of course, be released at once. In the meantime I shall send Laura THE NEEDFUL tomorrow without fail. At 41 Maitland Park[2] ALL RIGHT.
F. Engels
  1. ↑ Lafargue
  2. ↑ Marx's address in London
