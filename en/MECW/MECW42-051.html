Letter to Friedrich Engels, before February 22, 1865

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 22 February 1865


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 31, Moscow, 1963
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London, before 22 February 1865]

Dear FREDERICK.

Enclosed some secret contributions to the crème bonapartiste as a Sunday treat and pour la bonne bouche.[1]

You must keep these scraps of paper. MY COMPLIMENTS TO MRS LIZZIE.[2]

Your

K. M.

  1. ↑ as a special delicacy
  2. ↑ Lizzie Burns
