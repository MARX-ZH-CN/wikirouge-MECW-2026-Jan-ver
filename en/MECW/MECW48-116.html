Letter to Hermann Schluter, June 15, 1888

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 15 June 1888



First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 37, Moscow, 1965
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 48


ENGELS TO HERMANN SCHLÜTER
IN LONDON
London, 15 June 1888 122 Regent's Park Road
Dear Schlüter,
Will you and Tauscher do me the pleasure of taking luncheon with me on Sunday afternoon at half past two?
Your
F. Engels
