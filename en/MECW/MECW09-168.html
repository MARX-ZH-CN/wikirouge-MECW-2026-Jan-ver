Marx's Arrival in Paris

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 10 June 1849


First published in the Neue Kölnische Zeitung No. 132, June 10, 1849
Printed according to the newspaper
Published in English for the first time
Collection(s): Neue Kölnische Zeitung

Cologne, June 9. The editor-in-chief, Karl Marx, has arrived in Paris.[1]

  1. ↑ The news of Marx's arrival in Paris was evidently somewhat delayed in reaching Cologne. Judging from Marx's letter to Engels of July 7, 1849 sent from Paris to Kaiserslautern (the Palatinate), he arrived in the French capital in the first days of June.
