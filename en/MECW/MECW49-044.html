Letter to Amand Goegg, December 4, 1890

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 4 December 1890


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 37
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 49


ENGELS TO AMAND GOEGG

IN RENCHEN (BADEN)

London, 4 December 1890

Dear Goegg,

Many thanks for your kind good wishes. We old ones are beginning to be thin on the ground, a fact of which I have again been painfully reminded by the death of my beloved Lenchen. Well, I suppose there's bit of time left and I hope to make proper use of it.

Your old friend

F. Engels
