From the Minutes of the Committee Meeting of the Cologne Workers' Association on November 6, 1848. Entry Relating to Marx's Report on the Events in Vienna

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 12 November 1848


First published in the newspaper Freiheit‚ Brüderlichkeit‚ Arbeit No. 6, November 12, 1848
Printed according to the newspaper
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 7
Collection(s): Freiheit‚ Brüderlichkeit‚ Arbeit

... The President, Dr. Marx, gave a short report on the events in Vienna and stressed especially that it was only as a result of the manifold betrayal on the part of the bourgeoisie there that it became possible for Windischgrätz to capture the city....
