Letter from Karl Marx to Jenny Longuet, August 20, 1881

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 20 August 1881



First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 50, Moscow 1981
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46


MARX TO JENNY LONGUET
IN ARGENTEUIL
[London,] 20 August 1881
41 Maitland Park Road, N.W.
My Sweetheart,
You may find it difficult to make head or tail of the above scrawl. But in effect, Mama and Helen arrived safely last night.
