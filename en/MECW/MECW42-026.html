Letter to Friedrich Engels, December 22, 1864

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 22 December 1864


First published in MEGA, Abt. III, Bd. 3, Berlin, 1930
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 22 December 1864

Dear FREDERICK,

In great haste. Please send Becker's letter back to me.[1] I hope you have not lost it.

Happy New Year!

Your

K. M.

  1. ↑ See this volume, pp. 52,53.
