Letter to Witold Jodko-Narkiewicz, December 14, 1894

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 14 December 1894


First published, in the language of the original, in: Z Pola walki, No. 4 (12), 1960
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


ENGELS TO WITOLD JODKO-NARKIEWICZ

IN LONDON

[London,] 14 December 1894
41 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Dear Jodko,

I certainly would not have the least objection to your translating my article on the peasant question and should be glad if it could also prove helpful to your compatriots.[1]

Yours,

F. Engels

As you see, we have moved house.

  1. ↑ On 13 December 1894 Witold Jodko-Narkiewicz asked Engels for permission to translate his work The Peasant Question in France and Germany (present edition, Vol. 27) for the Polish journal Przedsmit. The translation was published in No. 12 of this journal for 1894.
