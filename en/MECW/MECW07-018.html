The Reaction (Neue Rheinische Zeitung)

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 5 June 1848



Source: Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 7, p. 45;
First published: in Neue Rheinische Zeitung No. 6, June 6, 1848.
Collection(s): Neue Rheinische Zeitung

Cologne, June 5. The dead ride fast. [Gottfried Bürger, "Lenore"] Herr Camphausen disavows the revolution and the reaction dares to suggest to the Agreement Assembly that the revolution should be stigmatised as a riot. On June 3, a deputy [Karl Richter] moved that a monument be erected for the soldiers who died on March 18.
