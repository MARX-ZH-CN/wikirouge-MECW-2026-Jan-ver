Letter to Karl Kautsky, July 22, 1887

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 22 July 1887


First published in Aus der Frühzeit des Marxismus. Engels Briefwechsel mit Kautsky, Prag, 1935, and in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVII, Moscow, 1935
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 48


ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN VENTNOR

London, 22 July 1887

Dear Kautsky,

Leaving tomorrow. Address 4 Cavendish Place, Eastbourne.[1]

Herewith a package of newspapers.[2] Greetings from us all. You're not too hot I hope!

Here super-shirt-sleeve weather.

Your

F. E.

  1. ↑ Engels holidayed in Eastbourne from 23 July to 2 September.
  2. ↑ Bürger Zeitung
