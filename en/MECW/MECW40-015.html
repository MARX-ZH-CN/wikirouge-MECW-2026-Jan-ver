MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 7 May 1856

28 Dean Street, Soho

Dear Frederic,

I have another letter from Germany to send on to you. But since you haven't yet acknowledged receipt either of the PARCEL or of the letter enclosing Miquel's,[1]I don't know whether these things have reached you. Let me know by return. In the case of compromising letters, it's essential to be sure they are delivered.

Salut.

Your

K. M.
