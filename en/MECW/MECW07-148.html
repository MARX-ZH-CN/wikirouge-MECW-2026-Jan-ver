Receipts of the Central Authority of the Communist League for Money Received and Paid Out

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Communist League (Marx-Engels)
Written 2 April 1848


First published in Der Bund der Kommunisten. Dokumente und Materialen, Bd. 1, 1836-1849, Berlin, 1970
Printed according to the manuscripts
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 7

All the three receipts are in Engels' handwriting.

I

April 2, 1848

Communist League

Workers of all countries, unite!

The Central Authority certifies that it has received the sum of twenty-five francs (f. c.[1]) from its member Karl Marx.

Paris, April 2, 1848

For the Central Authority

K. Marx

Engels

Henry Bauer

Joseph Moll

II

Communist League

Workers of all countries, unite!

The Central Authority has received an advance of seventy-four francs 20 centimes from its member Marx, which is hereby confirmed.

Paris, April 2, 1848

For the Central Authority

Engels

Bauer

J. Moll

III

Communist League

Workers of all countries, unite!

League member Friedrich Crüger acknowledges that he has received the sum of twenty-five francs as an advance from the Central Authority and he promises to repay this sum as soon as possible to the League.

Paris, April 2, 1848

Friedrich Crüger

  1. ↑ Fidei-commissum.— Ed.
