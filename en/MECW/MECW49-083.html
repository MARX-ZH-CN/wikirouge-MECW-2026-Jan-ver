Letter to Pasquale Martignetti, March 6, 1891

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 6 March 1891


First published in the language of the original (German), in La corrispondenza di Marx e Engels con italiani, 1848-1895, Milano, 1964
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 49


ENGELS TO PASQUALE MARTIGNETTI

IN BENEVENTO

London, 6 March 1891

Many thanks for Critica Sociale.[1]Sig. Avv.[2]Turati had already sent it to me direct and promises to send it regularly. Trust that your prospects are brighter. I am anxious to see the translation of the Communist Manifesto.

Warm regards from

Your

F.E.

  1. ↑ This refers to the opposition group of the Jungen (see Note 13).
