Letter to Friedrich Engels, November 9, 1867

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 9 November 1867


First published in MEGA, Abt. III, Bd. 3, Berlin, 1930
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 9 November 1867

DEAR FRED,

Enclosed from Kugelmann. Salut.

Your

Moor

[Postscript from Laura Marx in English]

Dear Engels,

As Mohr's Secretary I am really rather ashamed to send off a letter of this kind.

Yours sincerely

Kakadou[1]

  1. ↑ Laura's jocular nickname.
