Minutes of Engels' Lecture to the London German Workers' Educational Society on December 7, 1847

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 7 December 1847



Source: Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 6, p. 627;
First published in Archiv für die Geschichte des Sozialismus und der Arbeiterbewegung, Tg. 8, Leipzig 1919
Printed according to the Archiv...
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 6
Collection(s): Archiv für die Geschichte des Sozialismus und der Arbeiterbewegung

Citizen Engels gives a lecture in which he proves that commercial crises are caused only by overproduction and that the stock exchanges are the main offices where proletarians are made.
