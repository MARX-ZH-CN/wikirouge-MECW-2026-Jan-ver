Letter to Friedrich Engels, November 8, 1868

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 8 November 1868


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 4, Stuttgart, 1913
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 43


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 8 November 1868

SECOND LETTER. Postscriptum. I have just come from Borkheim's. To my surprise I have managed to get him not to print your 2 articles[1]under his patronage.

Your

K. M.

  1. ↑ F. Engels, 'Democratic Pan Slavism', see also this volume, pp. 153, 154.
