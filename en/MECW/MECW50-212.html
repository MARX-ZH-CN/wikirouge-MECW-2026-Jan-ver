Letter to Carl Hirsch, November 8, 1894

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 8 November 1894


Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


ENGELS TO CARL HIRSCH

IN COLOGNE

[London,] 8 November 1894

Dear Hirsch,

While thanking you very much for your regular despatch of the Rheinische Zeitung, I would ask you to be so kind as to change the address from 122 to No. 41, Regent's Park Road, London, N. W. It is on the

other side of the road, but at the bottom of Primrose Hill and closer to the entrance to the Park. All well in other respects.

Yours,

F.E.
