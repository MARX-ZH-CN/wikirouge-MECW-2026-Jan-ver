Letter to Friedrich Engels, October 19, 1865

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 19 October 1865


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 3, Stuttgart, 1913
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 19 October 1865

DEAR FRED,

I shall arrive in Manchester ABOUT 4.40 tomorrow afternoon and will make my way to your official dwelling.[1]

Your

K. M.

  1. ↑ Marx stayed with Engels in Manchester from 20 October to early November 1865.
