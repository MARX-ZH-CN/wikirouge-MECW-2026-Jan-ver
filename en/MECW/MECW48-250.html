Letter to Hermann Schluter, January 15, 1890

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 15 January 1890


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 37, Moscow, 1965
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 48


ENGELS TO HERMANN SCHLÜTER[1]

IN NEW YORK

[London,] 15 January 1890

Burns has sent us word that he does not know the man concerned,[2] so the firm is at any rate an obscure one.[3]

A great deal of flu around - so far we've been spared. Otherwise nothing new.

Your

F. E.

  1. ↑ These lines were on a post card, with the following address at the back: H. Schluter Esq., 73, First Avenue, New York City, US America.
  2. ↑ George W. Reid
  3. ↑ See this volume, p.436
