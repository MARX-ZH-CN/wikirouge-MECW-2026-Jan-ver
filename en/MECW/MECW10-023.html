Editorial Note (Neue Rheinische Zeitung Revue)

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written April 1850


Source: Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 10, p. 348;
Written: second half of April, 1850;
First published: the Neue Rheinische Zeitung Politisch-ökonomische Revue No. 4, April, 1850.
Collection(s): Neue Rheinische Zeitung. Politisch-ökonomische Revue

We have received the following report from Washington: "Herr Didier, editor of the New-Yorker Schnellpost, claims that he was formerly on the editorial staff of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung." We hereby declare that this is untrue.
