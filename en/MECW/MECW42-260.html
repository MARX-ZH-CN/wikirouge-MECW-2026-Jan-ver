Letter to Friedrich Engels, September 13, 1867

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 13 September 1867


First published in the language of the original, English, in MEGA, Abt. III, Bd. 3, Berlin, 1930
Reproduced from the photocopy of the telegram
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO ENGELS[1]

IN MANCHESTER

[Telegram]

London, 13 September 1867

From Karl Marx, 1 Modena Villas, Maitland Park. To Fred Engels, Southgate, St. Mary's.

Will start from Euston station 4.15 p.m.

  1. ↑ From 13 September 1867, Marx and Paul Lafargue stayed for a few days with Engels in Manchester.
