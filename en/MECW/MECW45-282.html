Letter to Karl Marx, after October 8, 1879

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 8 October 1879


First published in MEGA, Abt. III, Bd. 4, Berlin, 1931
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


ENGELS TO MARX

IN LONDON

[London, after 8 October 1879]

Didn't you send the noble Barry the notes I put into Fraser?[1] I put them there under Pumps' own eyes and in such a way that they were visible from outside. If not, send them off to him.

Your

F. E.
