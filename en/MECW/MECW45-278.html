Letter to Carl Hirsch, September 18, 1879

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 18 September 1879


First published in: Marx/Engels, Werke, Bd. 39, Berlin, 1973. Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


MARX TO CARL HIRSCH[1]

IN LONDON

[London,] 18 September 1879

Dear Hirsch,

Arrived in London![2]

Greetings,

Yours

K. M.

  1. ↑ This note is written on a postcard addressed to: 'Carl Hirsch, Esq., 12 Fitzroy Street, Fitzroy Square, W.'
  2. ↑ On 21 August 1879, Marx interrupted his stay in Jersey (see Note 494) and n arrived in Ramsgate to join his daughter Jenny and her newborn son Edgar. He returned to London on 17 September.
