Letter to Karl Marx, October 22, 1869

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 22 October 1869


Dear Moor,

In all haste have enclosed the photographs, of which Jenny should select one. I have a bad grippe because of the great change in temperature, but it is starting to get better.

The book by Wakefield[1] is cited by Butt[2] as A View of Ireland and also An Account of Ireland, 2 volumes, 1812 or 1813.

Young's book is called: A Tour in Ireland, 2 volumes, date not given.

Ranc's novel[3] is very nice.

Best greetings.

Your
F. E.

First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 4, Stuttgart, 1913
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time
