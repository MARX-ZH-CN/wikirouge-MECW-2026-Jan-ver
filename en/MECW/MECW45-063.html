Letter to Pyotr Lavrov, October 8, 1875

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 8 October 1875


First published, in Russian, in Letopisi marksizma, Book V, Moscow-Leningrad, 1928
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


MARX TO PYOTR LAVROV[1]

IN LONDON

[London,] 8 October 1875
41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.

Dear Friend,

I am sorry that I and my wife were not at home when you were kind enough to call.

I got the Quelques Mots d'un Groupe... Russe etc. from Engels[2] and shall, with your permission, show it to another of my friends before returning it. This puerile scriptum does not warrant an answer.

Yours sincerely,

K. M.

  1. ↑ Marx wrote this letter on a postcard. On the side reserved for the address he wrote: 'Mons. P. Lawroff, 3, Evershot Road, Tollington Park, Holloway.'
  2. ↑ See this volume, p. 91.
