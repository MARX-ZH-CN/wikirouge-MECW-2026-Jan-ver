First published in MEGA Abt. III, Bd. 4, Berlin, 1931
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 43


MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 12 December 1869

DEAR FRED,

Just before the close of post I saw the letter from the Solingen people lying next to me; so I had forgotten to enclose it for you yesterday, which I do herewith/

Also a proof, sent me by Beesly, but the continuation of it is missing. The page with the portraits—Paris—belongs to Jen- nychen, so you must return it after PERUSAL.

Salut.

Your

Moor

Various resolutions will be taken about the Bee-Hive, d'abord[1] in order to register them in our Minutes Book, and then to publish them when there is an opportunity?
