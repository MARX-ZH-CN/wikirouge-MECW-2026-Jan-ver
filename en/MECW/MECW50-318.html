Letter to Filippo Turati, July 4, 1895

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 4 July 1895


First published, in Italian, in: Filippo Turati attraverso le lettere di corrispondenti (1880-1925), Bari, 1947
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


ENGELS TO FILIPPO TURATI

IN MILAN

Eastbourne, 4 July 1895

4 Royal Parade

Letter received. All the best. Goodbye.

F. Engels
