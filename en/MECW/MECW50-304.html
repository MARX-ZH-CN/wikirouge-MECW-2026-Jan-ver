Letter to Ignaz Brand, May 20, 1895

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 20 May 1895


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 50, Moscow, 1981
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


ENGELS TO IGNAZ BRAND

IN VIENNA

London, 20 May [18]95
41 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Esteemed Comrade, many thanks for kindly sending the two copies of Námezdni Prace a Kapital,[1] one of which I have given to Mrs Eleanor Marx Aveling. If she does not learn Czech now, I am not to blame. Please give my thanks to the translator[2] with the assurance that my progress in Czech, though slow, is, I hope, none the less steady for it.

Friendly greetings to Mr Heller.

Yours,

F. Engels

  1. ↑ K. Marx, Wage Labour and Capital
  2. ↑ J. K. Náchodsky
