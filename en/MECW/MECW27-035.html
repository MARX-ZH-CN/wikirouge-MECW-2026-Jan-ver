Letter to the Committee of the Communist German Workers' Educational Society

Author(s) Frederick Engels
Written 1 December 1891


First published in Internationale wissenschaftliche Korrespondenz zur Geschichte der deutschen Arbeiterbewegung, No. 10, Berlin 1970
