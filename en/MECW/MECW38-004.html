Letter to Heinrich Börnstein, Not later than November 1844

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written November 1844


Source: Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 38, p. 8;
Written: not later than November 1844
First published: Ishihama Tomoyuki, In the Trace of Battles, Tokyo, 1926
Collection(s): The Trace of Battles

To Heinrich Börnstein in Paris

Dear Sir,

Return the Feuerbach sheets [L. Feuerbach, Das Wesen des Glaubens im Sinne Luther's] to me as soon as you have printed them.[1]

Yours faithfully

Marx

  1. ↑ L. Feuerbach's Das Wesen des Glaubens im Sinne Luther's was published ill instalments in Vorwärts! from the middle of August to the end of October 1844.
