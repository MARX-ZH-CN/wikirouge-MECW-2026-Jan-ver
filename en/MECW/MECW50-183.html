Letter to Witold Jodko-Narkevich, June 5, 1894

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 5 June 1894



First published, in the language of the original (French), in the magazine Z pola walki, No. 4 (12), 1960
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


ENGELS TO WITOLD JODKO-NARKEVICH
IN LONDON
[London,] 5 June 1894
122 Regent's Park Road, N. W.
My dear Jodko, I cannot recall exactly whether you have translated and published in Przedswit my article Eine polnische Proklamation (Internationales, aus dem 'Volksstaat' 370). If you have, please send me another copy of Przedswit with this article; some Polish students from Vienna have asked me for permission to translate it.[1]
Yours
F. Engels
