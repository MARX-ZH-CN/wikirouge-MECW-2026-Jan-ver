Letter to John Hales, October 14, 1871

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 14 October 1871


First published, in Russian and (in facsimile) in English, in Londonskaya konferentsia Pervogo Intematsionala (The London Conference of the First International), Moscow, 1936
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 44


MARX TO JOHN HALES[1]

IN LONDON

[London,] 14 October 1871

Dear Hales,

You must copy the following Resolution0 and send it to the daily London papers (the English ones; Serraillier sends it to the French papers).

Yours fraternally,

Karl Marx

  1. ↑ Marx's letter to Hales was written on a General Council form together with the text of the resolution on Nechayev (see present edition, Vol. 23, p. 23). British newspapers did not publish the Nechayev resolution.
