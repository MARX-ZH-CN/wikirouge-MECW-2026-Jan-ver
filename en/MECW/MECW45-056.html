Letter to Max Oppenheim, September 6, 1875

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 6 September 1875


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVI, Moscow 1935
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


MARX TO MAX OPPENHEIM

IN PRAGUE

Karlsbad, 6 September 1875 Germania

Dear Friend,

Having had no reply to the letter I sent you last week, I conclude that you are not in Prague. On that assumption, I shall not return home via Prague on Saturday (11th September) but direct via Frankfurt.

Au revoir,

Your most sincere friend,

Karl Marx
