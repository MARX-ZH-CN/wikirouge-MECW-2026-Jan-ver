Letter to Roland Daniels, December 31, 1881

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 31 December 1881


First published in German and in Russian in: Karl Marx, Frederick Engels, Collection of Photographs, Moscow, 1976
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46


MARX TO ROLAND DANIELS

IN COLOGNE

To Mr Roland Daniels with compliments

Karl Marx

Ventnor (Isle of Wight),
31 December 1881

My daughter[1]happened to bring this photogram with her when we left London; she told me of this when my letters to your esteemed mother[2]and yourself had already been despatched. Hence this is being sent later.
