Letter to Mrs James M. Williamson, January 11, 1883

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 11 January 1883


Published for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46. Reproduced from a photocopy of the original

TO MRS JAMES M. WILLIAMSON IN VENTNOR

Ventnor, 11 January 1883

Madame James M. Williamson with the wishes for a happy New Year.

Karl Marx
