Letter to Asher & Co, February 13, 1872

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 13 February 1872


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 50, Moscow, 1981
Reproduced from the original

MARX TO ASHER & CO.[1]

IN LONDON

[London,] 13 February 1872

Dear Sir,

Together with these lines I send you the only copy of the 'Inaugural Address'[2] I can still dispose of.

Yours truly,

Karl Marx

  1. ↑ This is a reply to the request from the firm Asher & Co. (contained in a letter of 12 February 1872) to send them a copy of the English edition of the Inaugural Address of the Working Men's International Association.
    This letter was published in English for the first time in Unbekanntes von Friedrich Engels und Karl Marx. Part I: 1840-1874. Ed. by Bert Andreasjacques Grandjonc, Hans Pelger. Papers of the Karl-Marx-Haus, Trier, 1986.
  2. ↑ K. Marx, 'Inaugural Address of the Working Men's International Association'.
