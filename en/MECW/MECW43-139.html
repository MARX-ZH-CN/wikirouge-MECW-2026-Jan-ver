Letter to Friedrich Engels, December 23, 1868

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 23 December 1868



First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 4, Stuttgart, 1913
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 43


MARX TO ENGELS
IN MANCHESTER
[London,] 23 December 1868
DEAR FRED,
I simply acknowledge—since I must go to the City right away—the receipt of your brilliant CHRISTMAS present. You can imagine what jubilation there was in the house.
Salut.
Your
K. M.
