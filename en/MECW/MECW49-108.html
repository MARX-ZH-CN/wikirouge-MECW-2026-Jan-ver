Letter to Franz Mehring, June 5, 1891

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 5 June 1891


Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 49


ENGELS TO FRANZ MEHRING

IN BERLIN

London, 5 June 1891

Kapital und Presse[1] received with thanks. I wish it every success. I didn't know your address, hence belated reply.[2]

  1. ↑ F. Mehring, Kapital und Presse. Ein Nachspiel zum Falle Lindau
  2. ↑ Engels wrote this note on his visiting card.
