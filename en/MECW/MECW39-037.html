Letter to Friedrich Engels, April 5, 1852

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 5 April 1852



First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 1, Stuttgart, 1913
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 39


ENGELS TO MARX
IN LONDON
[Manchester, 5 April 1852]
Should I not finish the article for Dana this evening, it will be chiefly because I still have to go through the better part of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung for April and March 1849,[1] for on this occasion the Frankfurters[2] must be given a thorough lambasting. The Bauer[3] is not adequate for the purpose.
Your
F. E.
  1. ↑ A reference to the material for Revolution and Counter-Revolution in Germany.
  2. ↑ former deputies to the Frankfurt National Assembly of 1848-49
  3. ↑ B. Bauer, Der Untergang des Frankfurter Parlaments.
