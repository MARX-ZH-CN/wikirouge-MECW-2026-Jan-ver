Letter to Friedrich Engels, December 18, 1882

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 18 December 1882



First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 4, Stuttgart, 1913
Printed according to the original in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46


MARX TO ENGELS
IN LONDON
[Ventnor,] 18 December 1882
DEAR FRED,
I return the ms.[1] ; very good! The doctor[2] has just left; I can't say that I notice any PROGRESS, rather the reverse. Though not cold out of doors, it is damp and rainy and the doctor says he cannot allow me to go out until the next fine day; otherwise he wouldn't accept the responsibility.
Au diable! il faut patienter![3] Salut.
Moor
  1. ↑ F. Engels, 'The Mark'.
  2. ↑ James M. Williamson
  3. ↑ Drat it! One must be patient!
