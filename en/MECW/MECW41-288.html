Letter to Friedrich Engels, June 10, 1863

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 10 June 1863



First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 3, Stuttgart, 1913
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 41


MARX TO ENGELS
IN MANCHESTER
[London,] 10 June 1863
Dear Engels,
A bill [of exchange] (the BUTCHER'S) on myself for £6 falls due next Monday (15 June). If you can let me have the money by then, it could be returned to you later, as soon as the transaction with Dronke has been completed."
Salut.
Your
K. M.
What do you make of La France, EH? And Poland? And our 'valiant compatriots' in Berlin?
