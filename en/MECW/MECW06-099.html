Announcement of the Regular Meeting of the German Workers' Club in Paris

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 13 March 1848



Printed according to the newspaper
Translated from the French
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 6

German Workers' Club—Meeting on Tuesday, March 14, at the café Picard, 93, rue Saint-Denis. Those who wish to join the club are asked to give their names to:
Hermann, rue du Faubourg St. Antoine 55, c/o M. Scheuffer;
Stumpf, rue Montorgueil 33, c/o M. Schard;
Seidel, rue Oblin 9, c/o M. Glass;
L. Etrich, rue Saint-Marc 5;
Charles Marx,[1] rue Neuve-Ménilmontant 10;
Charles Schapper, rue Neuve Saint-Augustin 22.
  1. ↑ The original has a misprint: Clarck.—Ed.
