Letter to Karl Kautsky, June 15, 1887

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 15 June 1887


First published in Aus der Frühzeit des Marxismus. Engels Briefwechsel, mit Kautsky, Prag, 1935 and in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVII, Moscow, 1935
Published in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 48

To Kautsky in London

London, 15 June 1887

Dear Kautsky,

On what day did you write to Liebknecht about la Schack? I've just had a letter from Mrs Liebknecht from which it appears that she, at any rate, knows nothing at all about the matter. Would like to clear up the mystery.

Your

F. E.
