Decisions of the General Meeting of the Cologne Workers' Association Held on April 23, 1849

Author(s) Karl Marx
Karl Schapper
Wilhelm Wolff
Written 29 April 1849


First published in the newspaper Freiheit, Brüderlichkeit, Arbeit No. 24, April 29, 1849
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 9
Collection(s): Freiheit‚ Brüderlichkeit‚ Arbeit

1. The general meeting will be held in future every Wednesday.

2. The provisional committee, consisting of Karl Marx, Karl Schapper, Wilhelm Wolff, Friedrich Anneke, Esser and Otto, elected by the Committee for the purpose of convening here in Cologne a congress of the Workers' Associations of the Rhineland and Westphalia, is confirmed.
