Letter to Karl Marx, August 21, 1862

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 21 August 1862


First published in Der Briefwechsel zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 3, Stuttgart, 1913
Printed according to the original
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 41


ENGELS TO MARX

IN L O N D O N

[Manchester, 21 August 1862]

Dear Moor,

The enclosed note for Lassalle.c Don't get so worked up about these asininities. Just see that the bill comes back, and send it to Lassalle for acceptance; or have this done direct from Berlin. You must realise that I can no more prevail on Borkheim than you can—probably less. I know he likes to show off.

In great haste.

Your

F. E.
