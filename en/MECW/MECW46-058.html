Letter from Karl Marx to Friedrich Adolph Sorge, June 2, 1881

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 2 June 1881


First published in The New-York Herald, No. 16455, 10 September 1881
Reproduced from the newspaper in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 46


MARX TO FRIEDRICH ADOLPH SORGE

IN HOBOKEN

London, 2 June 1881

Dear Sorge,

I recommend to you most heartily the bearer of this card, my friend Hartmann.
