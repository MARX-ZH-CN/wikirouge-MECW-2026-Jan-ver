Letter to Hermann Schluter, January 31, 1889

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written 31 January 1889


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 37, Moscow, 1965
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 48


ENGELS TO HERMANN SCHLÜTER

IN LONDON

[London], 31 January 1889

Dear Schlüter,

I had hoped to see you and your wife[1]here yesterday. Did Mrs Bernstein come with you? If so, I hope to see her and you both at my house one evening—Sunday at the latest. Kindest regards.

Your

F. Engels

  1. ↑ Anna Schlüter
