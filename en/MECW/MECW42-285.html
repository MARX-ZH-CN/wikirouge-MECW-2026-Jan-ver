Letter to Carl Siebel, November 10, 1867

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 10 November 1867


First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXV, Moscow, 1934. Translated from the Russian. Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 42


MARX TO CARL SIEBEL[1]

IN MADEIRA

London, 10 November 1867

Dear Siebel,

For the time being I am sending you this personal copy of mine.[2]At the same time I wrote to Meissner, the deuce take him! I

[3]

had actually instructed him to send you the first copy to appear in Germany. I hope that Madeira will do you good.

Karl Marx
