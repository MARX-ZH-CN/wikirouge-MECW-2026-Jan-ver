Letter to Enrico Bignami, Editor of La Plebe

Author(s) First International
Frederick Engels
Written 13 October 1871


This letter was published in English for the first time in The General Council of the First International. 1870-1871. Minutes, Progress Publishers, Moscow, 1967.
Source : Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 23

London, October 13 [1871]

Citizen Editor of La Plebe,

The General Council has entrusted me with sending you the enclosed resolution[1]and requesting that you publish it in the columns of your esteemed newspaper.

I remain yours sincerely,

Frederick Engels,

Secretary for Italy

  1. ↑ F. Engels, "Resolution of the General Council Expelling Gustave Durand from the International Working Men's Association" (see this volume, p. 21).— Ed
