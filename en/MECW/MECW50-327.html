Letter to an Unknown Correspondent, end December 1892

Author(s) Friedrich Engels
Written December 1892



First published in Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 38, Moscow, 1965
Reproduced from the original
Published in English in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 50


TO AN UNKNOWN CORRESPONDENT
[London, end of December 1892]
Hail to eighteen
ninety three!
Hope and joy dawn
with it newly.
Bright and happy
may it be To the
end, prays
yours most truly
F.E.
