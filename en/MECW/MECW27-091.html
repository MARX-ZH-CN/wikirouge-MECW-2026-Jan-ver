Letter to the Committee of the Independent Labour Party, 1895

Author(s) Frederick Engels
Written 16 April 1895



Written after April 16, 1895
First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXIX, Moscow, 1946
Reproduced from the draft manuscript
Source : Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 27

The Committee of the Independent Labour Party (see Note 434) invited Engels to the May Day Socialist Carnival in a letter of April 16, 1895.

Best thanks to the Committee for its kind invitation, but as my medical adviser strongly objects just now to my accepting every engagement of any sort I am compelled to decline it. As the ticket may be useful for some one else I take the liberty of returning it herewith.
