Letter to Friedrich Engels, September 14, 1879

Author(s) Karl Marx
Written 14 September 1879


First published in MEGA, Abt. III, Bd. 4, Berlin, 1931
Published in English for the first time in Marx-Engels Collected Works, Volume 45


MARX TO ENGELS

IN LONDON

[Ramsgate,] 14 September 187952S

Dear FRED,

Tell Tussy that she is invited to 2 o'clock dinner at Jenny's on Sunday and to a display of Edgar 'Marcel' Longuet who was today registered a British citizen at Ramsgate.

For Jennychen and suite leave for London tomorrow; ditto Helene[1] to give her SUPPORT.

CHEQUE encashable Monday. I have discovered another letter from Hirsch to myself which belongs in the dossier[2] of the case; I enclose it.

Salut.

Your

Moor

  1. ↑ Helene Demuth
  2. ↑ Marx is referring to the correspondence over the publication of the newspaper Der Sozialdemokrat.
