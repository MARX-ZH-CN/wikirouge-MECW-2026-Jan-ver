Marx in Neue Rheinische Zeitung October 1848

Editorial Statement concerning the Reappearance of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung

Source: MECW Volume 7, p. 456;
Written: by Marx on October 11, 1848;
First published: in the Neue Rheinische Zeitung No. 114, October 12, 1848.

Due to the interest shown, particularly in Cologne, for the preservation of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung, we have been able to overcome the financial difficulties brought about by the state of siege and to let the paper reappear. The editorial board remains the same. Ferdinand Freiligrath has newly joined it.

Karl Marx
Editor-in-Chief of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung
