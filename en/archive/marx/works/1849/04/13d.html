Engels in Neue Rheinische Zeitung April 1849

A Magyar Victory

Source: MECW Volume 9, p. 267;
Written: by Engels on April 12, 1849;
First published: in the Neue Rheinische Zeitung No. 271 (second edition), April 13, 1849.

Cologne, April 12. We are publishing a second edition today, not to inform our readers of yesterday’s basically quite unimportant debates at Frankfurt but to report something much more important, namely that the Magyars have gained a significant victory over the imperial army and that the imperial army, beaten all along the line, has withdrawn to below the walls of Pest. (See “Hungary”)[219]
