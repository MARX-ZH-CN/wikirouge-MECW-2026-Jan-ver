Anti-Dühring Engels 1877

On Thomas More

Abstract

From: Frederick Engels, Preparatory Writings for Anti-Dühring;
Source: Marx Engels On Literature and Art, Moscow 1976;
Transcribed: by Andy Blunden.

Hitherto, force — from now on, sociality. A pure pious wish, the demand for “Justice.” But Th. More made this demand 360 years ago, and it has yet to be fulfilled.
