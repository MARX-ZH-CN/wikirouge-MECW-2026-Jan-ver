Jenny Von Westphalen Marx (1814-1881)

Correspondence
1839-1869


Jenny Von Westphalen was born into a prominemt family in Trier and married Karl Marx after a lengthy engagement. Is said to have been an early influence on his politics and was involved with his work throughout her difficult life. Send corrections, comments and additions to Sally Ryan.
