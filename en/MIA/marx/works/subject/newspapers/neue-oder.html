Press Articles by Marx and Engels

 

Articles by Marx in

In the early 1850's, Karl Marx (and Frederick Engels, though to a lesser extent) wrote a quantity of journalist news summaries for the New-York Daily Tribune, the Neue Oder Zeitung and others. The articles listed here were published in the Neue Oder Zeitung, but not in the New York Tribune
