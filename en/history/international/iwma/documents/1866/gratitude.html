The International Workingmen's Association

Resolution of Gratitude to the Delegates of the Central (General) Council to the Geneva Congress
September 1866

Written: Adopted by the Central Council on September 18, 1866;
First published: in The Commonwealth, No. 185, September 22, 1866

Citizen Marx moved, and Dell seconded, a vote of thanks to the delegates for the able manner in which they had represented the Central Council at Geneva.
